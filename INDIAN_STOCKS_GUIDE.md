# Indian Stock Market Support in StockSense

## Overview
StockSense now supports Indian stocks listed on NSE (National Stock Exchange) and BSE (Bombay Stock Exchange) alongside US stocks with a **dedicated Indian Markets view**!

### **Dedicated Market Tabs**
The dashboard now has three dedicated views:
- **Global Overview**: See your watchlist and portfolio from all markets
- **US Markets**: S&P 500, Nasdaq, Dow Jones + popular US stocks
- **Indian Markets (NSE)**: NIFTY 50, Bank NIFTY, NIFTY IT + popular Indian stocks

Each market view shows:

## How to Use Indian Stocks

### 1. **Navigate to Indian Markets Tab**
Click on the "Indian Markets (NSE)" tab in the dashboard to see:
- NIFTY 50, Bank NIFTY, and NIFTY IT indices
- Top Indian stocks with live prices
- One-click access to stock details

### 2. **Search for Indian Stocks**
Indian stocks use specific suffixes:
- **NSE stocks**: Add `.NS` suffix (e.g., `RELIANCE.NS`, `TCS.NS`)
- **BSE stocks**: Add `.BO` suffix (e.g., `RELIANCE.BO`, `TCS.BO`)

### 2. **Market Selector**
Use the market selector dropdown in the header to filter your search:
- **All Markets**: Shows both US and Indian stocks
- **US Stocks**: Shows only US stocks
- **Indian Stocks (NSE)**: Shows only Indian stocks

### 3. **Search Examples**
Try searching for:
- `RELIANCE.NS` - Reliance Industries Ltd.
- `TCS.NS` - Tata Consultancy Services
- `HDFCBANK.NS` - HDFC Bank
- `INFY.NS` - Infosys
- `ICICIBANK.NS` - ICICI Bank

## Technical Details

### Data Source
- **Provider**: yfinance (Yahoo Finance)
- **Coverage**: All NSE and BSE listed stocks
- **Updates**: Real-time during market hours
- **Rate Limits**: Unlimited (no API key required)

### Symbol Format
- US Stocks: `SYMBOL` (e.g., `AAPL`, `MSFT`)
- Indian NSE Stocks: `SYMBOL.NS` (e.g., `RELIANCE.NS`)
- Indian BSE Stocks: `SYMBOL.BO` (e.g., `RELIANCE.BO`)

### Market Hours
- **NSE/BSE**: 9:15 AM - 3:30 PM IST (Monday-Friday)
- **US Markets**: 9:30 AM - 4:00 PM EST (Monday-Friday)

## Tips for Indian Investors

1. **Use NSE Symbols**: NSE is more liquid than BSE, so prefer `.NS` symbols
2. **Search Smart**: The search is case-insensitive, but results show proper format
3. **Check Market Badge**: Search results show which market each stock belongs to
4. **Portfolio Tracking**: Add your holdings with purchase price to track P&L
5. **News Filtering**: Filter news by specific Indian companies for targeted updates
